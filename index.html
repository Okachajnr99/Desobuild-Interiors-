Our Design Philosophy & Story

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by acclaimed designer Elena Desobuild, our firm began with a simple vision: to create interiors that not only look beautiful but also function seamlessly for the people who inhabit them.

Starting as a boutique residential design studio in Nairobi, we've grown into an internationally recognized firm with projects spanning luxury residences, corporate headquarters, and five-star hotels across East Africa and beyond.

Our Mission

To create interior environments that inspire, function flawlessly, and stand the test of time through innovative design, exceptional craftsmanship, and collaborative client partnerships.

Our Vision

To be Africa's foremost interior design firm that seamlessly blends artistic excellence with technical innovation across residential and commercial spaces.
